International School Social Worker

We Support Leaders, Advocates, and Champions.

Helping you track progress and stay organized

Social Workers

This website allows social workers to define and track any progress specific to student needs. The system allows for you to quickly, easily, and confidentially create a variety of reports. These reports can be customized meet specific grant requirements.

School Administrators

School Administrators will find the site easy to access and maintain. Intuitive security options allow for clear and well defined permission to be removed to granted with the click of a button. The same great reporting tools for social workers will be available for administrators as well, allowing for a variety of helpful report to be pulled.

Students

Students will most directly feel the impact for high quality data reporting. The concise and accurate progress reports give social workers and staff the tools needed to meet the individual needs in the most effective ways.The system allows for clear identification of areas of success and areas of need of improvement.
