Who We Serve

We serve any community and every community that feel that can benefit from our services. Our focus and purpose is to help improve low-resource communities by giving them the tools and knowledge to thrive and grow. Often times these schools don't have a way of tracking student progress without standardized tests and are often forced to have a high student to teach ratio. With our help, these low-resource communities can more easily track and metric of success and monitor their students well being.
